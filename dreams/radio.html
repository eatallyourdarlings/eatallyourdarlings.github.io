what’s that frequency?

it’s the frequency they dream at, and it’s never the same twice.

Rain wanders and picks up oneiric radio frequencies on the thingamabob. Through triangulation they piece together halves/fractions/fractals of strange signals. They start off just listening to Loqui’s favourite tunes but follow music to meet other folk. Everyone’s got a pirate radio station, how else are you meant to listen to music?
