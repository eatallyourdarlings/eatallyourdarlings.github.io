cast

every body is a bit traumatised

the world and story is mapped out by these characters (most often literally, through their dreams). So I know nothing of the world that they can’t know, or will know. Ironically, I’ve at times tried to define these characters by their trauma, while I think that’s exactly what all traumatised folk are trying to escape.

I don’t think they’d be known or referred to by the names or pronouns that I use for them in these notes - there’s a lot of nuance to their languages.

I think of them as actors all trying to disentangle their selves from stories written for them by other forces. Monolith is making sense of all these conflicting stories that yearn to join.
