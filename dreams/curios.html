curios

A strange and interesting object; something that evokes curiosity.

to be confused with thingamabobs.

interested in the complexity of curiosity, which is so often simplified into a harmless wonder, that has been used to justify violent practices like looting as naïve collecting.

flotsam from burnt down museums and alternate histories

Artefacts that have been or are in the process of being transmuted from organic to inorganic material, or vice versa. dubiofossils, ooparts. Axioms of a new alchemy; coral, plastic, sap, fibreglass, mycelium, iron, bone, stone.
