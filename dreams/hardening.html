hardening

ossify; calcify; fossilise; petrify

with all these sad half-alive guys I’m questioning the inevitability of hardening processes - material, emotional. maybe weathering is a more appropriate word. what is lost in the pursuit of a perfectly preserved final form?

on a meta-level, I worry that by hypertexting this world so much, the details of it will get so set in stone that I won’t have enough room for imagining new things. hence why I don’t want there to be too strict a canon.

Monolith is parasitising the planet’s slow systems; blurring geological lifecycles as we perceive them, distorting the line between lithosphere and biosphere and atmosphere and noosphere. Some eidola have adapted to this strange new reality, gaining the ability to petrify with songs, or with light. Petrification accelerates timescale, geologising in an instant.
