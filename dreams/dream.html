dream

dreams of a dead planet

On Mars, when people are sleeping or dead near Monolith, they join a collective dream, sharing memories and feelings.

In the coldest places, their collective trauma, despair and desensitisation imbrogliate into a nightmare. They’re unable to access the vital healing process of dreaming, and lose their selves. The strongest-willed dreamers have the greatest influence, like Val and Eris.

Monolith is using dreamers as a host to vicariously access timelost memories of homely red stars.
