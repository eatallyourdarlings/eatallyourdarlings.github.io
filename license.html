Frequently Asked Questions

What is meant by ‘feminist principles’? That is up to each person, collective or organization to decide. At the very least, it must have an aspiration to fight against cisheteropatriarchy and capitalism; and defend ecological, anti-racist, anti-capitalist and migrant justice positions.

What is cisheteropatriarchy? It is the system of social organisation that has the cisheterosexual men as the centre and measure of everything; establishing unequal power relations and oppression with the rest of human beings and nature.
