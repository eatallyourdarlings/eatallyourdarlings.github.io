eleven ladybugs and counting.

    things we saw on the beach…
  • three rust-bark chains,
  • three friendly fishers walking barefoot alongside us on the amalgam,
  • one rope ladder around four layers of barbed wire fences,
  • two metal detectors,
  • one stray driftwood arrow, approximately seven feet height, likely from an underwater skirmish,
  • eleven ladybugs crawling in the seafoam,
  • three bouquets of plastic flowers on a beachside grave, with a broken marble headstone.

Jackson said whenever he spots a ladybug it’s a rare and special moment. I said he should keep a count. We kept spotting more ladybugs on the beach, it felt like we’d cast a spell wrong. We’re still counting.
