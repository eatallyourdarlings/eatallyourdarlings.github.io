Little Guys is a platformer about a witch in a wheelchair trying to navigate a treacherous path to a tea party. To do so they enlist the help of a couple magical little guys who help make the level more accessible for them. It's the first game I finished, it's pretty janky and might not work in your browser.

audio

  • Heroyam Slava on SBS On Demand

Heroyam Slava is a short documentary film about my friend Sasha's time volunteering in a refugee camp in Poland, helping kids displaced by the ongoing conflict in Ukraine. It was directed by my friend Len and I was lucky enough to get to write music for it, which I am very proud of. I wrote a bunch of small motifs inspired by Sasha's stories, then Chloe the sound design wiz pieced them together.

  • old demos on Soundcloud

The demos include soundtracks I've made for my some more of my friends' films. Most were made on Garageband on my old iPhone. If you really like any of these tracks, please get in contact and I'll send them to you for free so that you can listen to them offline and my tunes can have less of a carbon impact from streaming.
