audio

  • Heroyam Slava on SBS On Demand

Heroyam Slava is a short documentary film about my friend Sasha's time volunteering in a refugee camp in Poland, helping kids displaced by the ongoing conflict in Ukraine. It was directed by my friend Len and I was lucky enough to get to write music for it, which I am very proud of. I wrote a bunch of small motifs inspired by Sasha's stories, then Chloe the sound design wiz pieced them together.

  • miscellania on Soundcloud

Soundcloud holds hostage various demos and some more soundtracks I've made for my friends' films. Most were made with Garageband on my old iPhone. If you really like any of these tracks, please get in contact and I'll send them to you for free so that you can listen to them offline and my tunes can have less of a carbon impact from streaming.

masked stinker

Made this guy in a haze at 1AM. It was my first time trying the #256fes challenge, which is making a tiny 3D model with less than 256 triangles and an image texture with 256 pixels. Modelled and rendered in Blender and then manually painted over with Bayer matrix patterns, shrinking the image to a measly 4 kilobytes and adding details. It was a lot of fun doing the 'work' of a computer algorithm by hand, inspired by Raquel Meyers' practice of kybdsl√∂jd.

NEWYMATION

NEWYMATION is a pretty groovy local artist meetup that happens every few months! I made this textmode poster with lvllvl dot com, using PETSCII, the limited character set of the Commodore 64. It features a fearsome rendition of The Roost mascot.

helping hands


Scrapped level concept for a 3D game jam, very much inspired by the Helping Hands of Labyrinth (1986), which gave me nightmares as a kid. Modelled and rendered in Blender, then made crunchy with dithering - algorithmic and by hand. This hand rig has come in handy many times (heh).
