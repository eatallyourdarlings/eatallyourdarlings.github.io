heroes

living other peoples’ stories

through their relationship, Cephi and Rain both come to realise how they were groomed into being heroes in other peoples’ stories.

Sometimes at night between the creased sheets of my mind 
The dead come back to life 
And provide advice 
Consoling ghosts 
Your dreams are the needed host 
To talk to those who were once close

They’re both a tabula rasa in a sense - Rain because of the way their trauma suppresses their memories, and Cephi by her moth-rebirth cycle. So they’re very susceptible to the dreams of others, in a time where it is believed that nobody can dream on their own anymore.
