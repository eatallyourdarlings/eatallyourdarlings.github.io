Why this license? I believe in the vital importance of the cultural commons as something that can help us transcend the division of labour, and I rebel against the absurd concept of intellectual property. This copyfarleft license allows cooperatives and solidarity-based collectives organising under ecofeminist ideals (or similar, e.g. solarpunk), but not corporations, to exploit cultural works for capital gain. It is a fork on the original text of the Creative Commons Non-Commercial license (CC BY-NC-SA) to make it explicitly anti-capitalist.

How would you like to be credited? With a short link to this website, http://orf.place/ as well as the date of retrieval (plus some nice words if you'd like). Also feel free to contact me with any questions and maybe even support me if you're feeling extra nice?

Frequently Asked Questions

What is meant by feminist principles? That is up to each person, collective or organization to decide. At the very least, it must have an aspiration to fight against cisheteropatriarchy and capitalism; and defend ecological, anti-racist, anti-capitalist and migrant justice positions.

What is cisheteropatriarchy? It is the system of social organisation that has the cisheterosexual men as the centre and measure of everything; establishing unequal power relations and oppression with the rest of human beings and nature.
