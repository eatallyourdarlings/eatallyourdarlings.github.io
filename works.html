games

  • orf dot itch dot io

I've put one janky little video game on the web, which you can play on my itch.io, but it may not work in your browser. It's called Little Guys and it's a platformer about a witch in a wheelchair trying to navigate a treacherous path to a tea party. To do so they enlist the help of a couple magical little guys who help make the level more accessible for them.

audio

  • Heroyam Slava on SBS On Demand
  • old demos on Soundcloud

Heroyam Slava is a short documentary film about my friend Sasha's time volunteering in a refugee camp in Poland, helping kids displaced by the ongoing conflict in Ukraine. It was directed by my partner Len and I was lucky enough to get to write music for it, which I am very proud of. I wrote a bunch of small motifs inspired by Sasha's stories, then Chloe the sound design wiz pieced them together.

I've put some old demos on my soundcloud, including soundtracks I've made for my friends' films for school. Most were made on Garageband on my old iPhone. I'm going to clean some of these up and share them here so I can take them off streaming (I'll probably use faircamp). If you really like any of the tracks on my soundcloud, please get in contact and I'll send them to you for free so that you can listen to them offline and my tunes can have less of a carbon impact.
