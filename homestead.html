homestead

stead; this place is not yours.
cross your lines, 
draw your legends
(your inkblot stars,
your fenced fields,
your authoritative ovals),
but do not lie to yourself
that you've mapped the territory.

call it home-stead
for a time
(no more than you deserve)
and know this:
this place cannot be yours.
